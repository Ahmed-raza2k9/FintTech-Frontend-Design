Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

Find quick answers to common questions about our platform, services, and support.

1. What kind of businesses do you work with?

We collaborate with startups, small-to-medium businesses, e-commerce brands, personal brands, and corporate companies. Whether you are launching a new venture or scaling up your web presence, we're ready to help.

2. What digital agency services do you offer?

We provide full-service digital solutions including custom website development, corporate websites, portfolio websites, landing pages, e-commerce storefronts, search engine optimization (SEO), social media management, graphic design, and modern UI/UX design.

3. How does the website design and development process work?

Our process starts with Discovery & Consultation to align on requirements, followed by Strategy & Planning. Next, we design custom UI/UX mockups, develop the website using high-performance tech stacks, and finally conduct rigorous testing before Launch & Growth support.

4. Do you integrate websites with third-party systems?

Yes, we build seamless integrations with major third-party platforms such as Stripe, PayPal, Salesforce, HubSpot, Mailchimp, custom databases, and external APIs to automate and streamline your operations.

5. Do you provide ongoing maintenance and technical support?

Absolutely. We offer comprehensive support packages that cover monthly security patches, speed optimization, CMS updates, regular backups, and content modifications to keep your website running at peak performance.

6. How long does it take to design and launch a website?

Timelines depend on the scale of the project. A custom landing page typically takes 1 to 2 weeks, a standard business website takes 3 to 4 weeks, and complex corporate portals or e-commerce platforms take 6 to 8 weeks.

7. Will my website be mobile-friendly and optimized for Google SEO?

Yes, every single layout we develop is fully responsive and mobile-friendly. Additionally, we integrate on-page SEO best practices (semantic HTML, fast load times, alt text, and meta tags) to help you rank on search engines.

8. What pricing packages do you offer?

We offer tailored plans: our Starter package begins at $199, Professional package at $499, and the Business package at $999. Custom pricing is also available for enterprise-level or complex custom web applications.
