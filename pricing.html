Pricing FAQs

Everything you need to know before committing — ask us anything.

1. Can I switch from one-time to a monthly retainer?

Yes, you can transition to a monthly retainer package at any time to receive ongoing updates, priority maintenance, continuous search engine optimization (SEO), and custom graphics support.

2. Do you offer discounts for startups or non-profits?

Absolutely. We offer custom discounts of up to 25% for registered non-profit organizations, educational systems, and early-stage bootstrapped startups. Reach out to us via our contact page to discuss your case.

3. How does the project payment structure work?

For one-time website design and development projects, we work on a standard 50% upfront deposit structure and the remaining 50% upon successful final launch. Monthly retainers are billed on the 1st of every month.

4. Is there a long-term commitment for monthly retainers?

No, there are no long-term contracts. Our monthly retainers are billed on a month-to-month basis, and you are free to upgrade, downgrade, pause, or cancel your subscription at the end of any billing cycle.

5. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers, and direct wire payments. All payments are processed securely with end-to-end encryption.
